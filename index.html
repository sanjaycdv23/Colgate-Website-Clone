Terms & Conditions

This Dental Screening Report is auto-generated using artificial intelligence, based on the dental screening images you uploaded, and/or the responses that you provided. The images are used to screen for visible caries and stains, and the questions evaluate the likelihood of gum problems. The Dental Score is calculated as a combination of the three oral conditions identified in the Report. The purpose of the Report as a dental screening tool is to promote understanding of general oral health. It does not constitute a medical diagnosis, and is not a substitute for professional diagnosis or treatment from a licensed dentist. We recommend you seek your dentist’s advice.

The technology used to assess your dental images is developed and maintained by Logy.AI (Nex Fitzap Private Limited) and Logy.AI is solely responsible for the functionality, effectiveness, and accuracy of that technology and the components of the report which are derived from it. The results of the dental image screening are subject to the quality of the images of your teeth. The screening tool is intended solely for the use of users who are citizens of India and should not be used by citizens of any other country.
